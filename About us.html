Our Story

Welcome to our kitchen, where the love for food brought three friends together on a culinary journey that sparked the creation of this delightful cooking blog.

We are Ariëlle, Lou, and Laura, the passionate trio behind the tantalizing recipes and heartwarming stories you'll find here. Our story begins with a shared love for exploring flavors and experimenting with ingredients.

We first crossed paths in a local cooking class, drawn together by the irresistible aroma of simmering spices and the joy of crafting a perfect dish. As we bonded over our mutual appreciation for culinary adventures, it became clear that our individual strengths and unique perspectives could blend into something extraordinary.

Laura, the spice enthusiast with a knack for bold flavors, brings her international palate to the table. From sizzling street food in Bangkok to the aromatic spices of Morocco, her adventurous spirit infuses our recipes with a world of tastes.

Lou, the baking virtuoso, discovered her passion for creating sweet masterpieces at an early age. Her desserts, ranging from classic cakes to innovative pastries, add a touch of sweetness to our culinary repertoire. Lily's meticulous attention to detail ensures that every confection is a work of art.

Ariëlle, the farm-to-table advocate, has a green thumb and a commitment to sustainable living. Her love for fresh, locally sourced ingredients inspires us to create dishes that not only taste amazing but also support local farmers and communities.

Our friendship blossomed in and out of the kitchen, and the idea of sharing our culinary adventures with the world soon sprouted. With a sprinkle of creativity, a dash of laughter, and a generous serving of friendship, we embarked on this blogging journey to invite you into our kitchen. Here, you'll find a diverse collection of recipes, each telling a story of shared moments, laughter, and the joy of creating delicious memories. From comforting classics to bold new experiments, we're thrilled to share our kitchen escapades with you. Join us as we whip, sauté, and bake our way through life, savoring every bite and cherishing the bonds that food has woven into our friendship.

So, grab a seat at our virtual table, and let's embark on a flavorful adventure together. Welcome to the heart and soul of our culinary world – welcome to our cooking blog!
